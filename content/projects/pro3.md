This project was funded by [BOYUXIN](http://www.byx-its.com).

Digital twin is a significant direction and application scenario for the development of informatization in the transportation, as well as a key visualization technique for smart city. The digital twin's technical advantages include digital identification, simultaneous visualization, virtual-real interaction, and intelligent control, and it will bring new solutions and avenues for intelligent parking management and intelligent driving.

In this project, my job includes
- Building up the parking lots on the SUMO simulation platform.
- Providing and visualize the information of parking occupancy in real-time.
