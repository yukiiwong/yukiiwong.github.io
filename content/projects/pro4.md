This project was funded by [Yangzhou Road Management Department](https://baike.baidu.com/item/扬州市公路管理处/21512466?fr=aladdin).

The intelligent traffic visualization application with large-scale data statistics and integration is the centralized statistics and display of data for each highway management platform subsystem. It may also increase the quality of expressway service, the quality of highway service, the stability of expressway traffic operation, and the dependability of the road network under adverse weather conditions. Future requirements for the rapid expansion of roadways can be met by an integrated application of intelligent traffic visualization with rich data content and intuitive display.

In this project, my job includes
- Designing the functions of the platform and divide it into flow management system, bridge maintenance system and road maintenance system.
- Visualizing data in each system using JavaScript.
