This project was funded by [School of Transportation in Southeast University](https://tc.seu.edu.cn/jt_en/).

Multiple causes, including the People's Hospital, contribute to severe traffic congestion in the Qingliang Mountain area of Gulou District in Nanjing. Consider using the underground area to construct a subway station and conduct a simulation-based traffic impact study. What type of crowd-gathering impact would the subway station be designed to create? What effect would a set of schemes, such as addresses, have on the road network after they are specified?

In this project, my job includes
- Build a simulation scene in Anylogic through 3D drawings of Qingliangshan subway station.
- Simulate the flow of people in the subway station and the pedestrian flow around the subway station in Anylogic.
