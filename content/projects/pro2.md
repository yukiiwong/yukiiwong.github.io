This project was funded by [HUAWEI](https://www.huawei.com/sg/).

The characteristics of the traffic system are dynamic and complicated. Traditional traffic simulation software can imitate the behavior of traffic participants, but it is a far cry from reality. By interacting with the environment, agents can acquire knowledge or enhance their performance via reinforcement learning techniques. According to the agent's predetermined objectives, in a specific environment and under a specific policy, specific actions are performed and incentives are acquired. Unlike traditional algorithms that rely on mathematical models, agent-based methods using reinforcement learning do not require prior information. In addition, it has excellent self-learning and self-adaptability for complex systems, and it can adapt its control strategy to system changes to satisfy the demands of dynamic and complicated traffic systems. By setting a realistic reward function, research on the traffic multi-agent simulation technology based on reinforcement learning enables ongoing interaction learning between the agent and the environment. Construct a multi-agent traffic simulation platform to generate novel solutions for urban traffic challenges and to optimize traffic management techniques.

In this project, my job includes
- Investigating the application of reinforcement learning in transportation and write a literature review.
- Coding a reinforcement learning algorithm in mode choice.
- Completinig the clustering work for large-scale decentralized decision making in transportation simulation.
